# Product Requirements Document (PRD) for The Big Bang Salon & Bridal Studio
## Overview
The Big Bang Salon & Bridal Studio is a unisex salon and bridal makeup service provider based in Vadodara. The goal of this project is to create a website that showcases the salon's services, provides an easy way for customers to book appointments, and establishes the brand as a leading hair and makeup service provider in the region.

## Business Goals
* Increase online visibility and reach a wider audience
* Showcase the salon's services and expertise
* Provide an easy way for customers to book appointments and inquire about services
* Increase customer engagement and loyalty
* Establish the brand as a leading hair and makeup service provider in Vadodara

## Target Audience
* Demographics: Women and men aged 18-50
* Interests: Beauty, fashion, hair care, makeup, weddings, and special occasions
* Location: Vadodara and surrounding areas
* Behaviors: People who value quality beauty services, are interested in trying new styles and trends, and are active on social media

## Pages Needed
* **Home**: Introduction to the salon, services, and team
* **Services**: Detailed list of hair, makeup, and bridal services offered
* **Portfolio**: Gallery of before-and-after photos, customer testimonials, and reviews
* **About Us**: Information about the salon's history, mission, and team
* **Contact**: Contact form, address, phone number, and email
* **Book an Appointment**: Online booking system for customers to schedule appointments
* **Bridal Services**: Dedicated page for bridal makeup and hair services
* **Blog**: News, tips, and trends in the beauty industry

## Features
* **Responsive Design**: Website should be optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
* **Online Booking System**: Integration with a booking system to allow customers to schedule appointments
* **Payment Gateway**: Integration with a payment gateway to allow customers to pay for services online
* **Social Media Integration**: Links to social media profiles and integration with social media feeds
* **Testimonials and Reviews**: Display of customer testimonials and reviews on the website
* **Gallery**: Photo gallery of services, before-and-after photos, and customer photos
* **Newsletter Signup**: Option for customers to sign up for a newsletter to receive updates and promotions
* **Search Engine Optimization (SEO)**: Optimization of the website for search engines to improve visibility and ranking

## Success Metrics
* **Website Traffic**: Increase in website traffic and engagement (e.g., time on site, pages per session)
* **Online Bookings**: Increase in online bookings and appointments scheduled
* **Customer Engagement**: Increase in customer engagement (e.g., social media likes, shares, comments)
* **Conversion Rate**: Increase in conversion rate (e.g., number of customers who book an appointment after visiting the website)
* **Customer Satisfaction**: Increase in customer satisfaction (e.g., positive reviews, testimonials)

## Technical Requirements
* **Content Management System (CMS)**: Website should be built using a CMS (e.g., WordPress, Joomla) to allow for easy updates and maintenance
* **Hosting**: Website should be hosted on a reliable and secure server
* **Backup and Security**: Regular backups and security updates should be performed to ensure the website's integrity and safety
* **Browser Compatibility**: Website should be compatible with major browsers (e.g., Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Safari)

## Timeline
* **Research and Planning**: 1 week
* **Design and Development**: 4-6 weeks
* **Testing and Quality Assurance**: 2-3 weeks
* **Launch**: 1 week

## Budget
* **Design and Development**: ₹50,000 - ₹100,000
* **Hosting and Maintenance**: ₹5,000 - ₹10,000 per year
* **Marketing and Promotion**: ₹10,000 - ₹20,000 per year

Note: The budget and timeline are estimates and may vary depending on the complexity of the project and the resources required.
